1- What is Nexus?

Nexus is a student hub designed to provide resources, tools, and a community for university students to collaborate, share materials, and find helpful academic and career-related content.

2- How can I access study notes?

To access study notes go to the Resources page, where you can find a variety of notes and study guides contributed by fellow students. You can also upload your own materials to help others.

3- How can I find previous exams for my courses on Nexus?

In the Resources page, you can access the section dedicated to "Previouses", and you will find a collection of past exams for various courses. Simply select your course, and you will see the available previouses uploaded by students.

4- Can I rate professors and courses on Nexus?

Yes! On the Ratings page, where you can read reviews from other students or submit your own rating to help others make informed decisions.

5- How do I find career opportunities?

You can find the latest internships, scholarships, and job opportunities on the Opportunities page, Additionally, Nexus offers career guidance through the Carrer Path Navigator section.

6- Are there any upcoming events or workshops?

Yes! the Events page lists all upcoming workshops, seminars, and networking events. You can also subscribe to updates to never miss an event.

7- How can I contact Nexus for support or questions?

You can reach out to us via email by visiting the Contact page or directly emailing us at contact@nexus.com.
